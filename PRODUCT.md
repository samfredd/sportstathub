# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Primary:** Sports bettors in Nigeria and the wider African market. Mostly on phones, often on the move, on variable networks, in the Africa/Lagos timezone. They currently mix intuition, insider tips, and fragmented stats to make selections, and they want fast access to booking codes and community insight at the moment of deciding a bet (pre-match).

**Secondary:** Pundits/creators who want to publish predictions and monetize their expertise; data-driven analysts who use the stats and self-prediction tools; international bettors interested in African leagues.

Their context is decision-making under time pressure before kickoff — they need the edge stated quickly and the action (copy a code, compare two teams, check a referee) one tap away.

## Product Purpose

A multi-sport predictions and analytics hub that aggregates reliable data and delivers transparent, data-driven predictions — football first, expanding to basketball, tennis, and more. Beyond predictions it provides: ready-made booking codes for major bookmakers (one-click copy + deep link), a community forum and comments, a creator/pundit ecosystem with revenue sharing, and AI-powered predictions with probability scores and confidence levels.

Benchmarked against Statof (deep, real-time football stats: goals, corners, cards, fouls, H2H, referee tendencies, Smart Analyse, Pro/Pro Max tiers) but **differentiated** by multi-sport coverage, ready-made booking codes, community interaction, and creator monetization — the gaps Statof leaves open.

Success looks like becoming the leading sports-predictions destination in Nigeria/Africa: a freemium model (free basic scores; Pro/Pro Max unlock full stats, unlimited H2H, AI Predict), funded by subscriptions, affiliate commissions on booking codes, and clearly-labeled bookmaker advertising. Year-one targets: ~50k active users, 100+ creators on a transparent revenue-share.

## Brand Personality

**Bold, sporty, data-confident.** Three words: decisive, numbers-forward, credible.

The voice states the edge plainly — odds, confidence, form — without hype or hedging. It reads like an expert teammate, not a screaming tipster. Energy comes from strong typography, a disciplined emerald + gold palette, and purposeful motion — never from ad banners, neon, popups, or gamification. It respects the user's intelligence.

Emotional goals: confidence, momentum, and trust.

## Anti-references

This must explicitly NOT look like:

- **Generic AI-generated SaaS.** Emerald-glow shadows, glassmorphism, gradient text, rainbow stat cards, Inter-for-everything, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section, cards-inside-cards. If it could be mistaken for a template generation, it has failed.
- **Cluttered legacy betting sites.** Banner-heavy, neon, aggressive popups and ad takeovers that bury the content — the typical bookmaker aesthetic.
- **Bland data-table tools.** Sparse, gray, spreadsheet-like (Statof-style) — data with no identity, energy, or hierarchy.
- **Gamified casino aesthetics.** Coins, confetti, slot-machine flourishes, childish gamification.

## Design Principles

1. **Transparency earns the click.** Every prediction shows its basis — probability, confidence, the stats behind it — and frames picks as informed suggestions, never guarantees. Trust is the actual product.
2. **Thumb-first, Lagos-first.** Designed for one-handed mobile use on variable networks: fast, light, legible in bright sun and in the dark. The phone is the primary device, not a shrink of the desktop.
3. **Confident, never loud.** Sporty energy through type, color discipline, and motion — not through banners, neon, or gamification. Stand apart from cluttered bookmaker sites by saying more with less.
4. **Identity through craft.** Avoid both the bland-data-tool trap and the AI-template trap by committing to one disciplined brand and shipping real polish — colour that carries meaning, real elevation, actually-loaded fonts.
5. **Community and creators are first-class.** Booking codes, forum, and pundit profiles are the differentiator, not bolt-ons — give them the same data-grade rigor and visual care as the match pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA.** Body text ≥4.5:1, large/bold text ≥3:1, placeholder text ≥4.5:1 (no light-gray-for-elegance). When contrast is close, bump toward the ink end of the ramp.
- **Reduced motion is not optional.** Every animation needs a `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` alternative (crossfade or instant).
- **Mobile-first & resilient.** Responsive from small screens up; minimal page weight and caching for variable African networks; one-handed reach.
- **Dark mode is first-class** (eye strain + battery), not an afterthought.
- **Responsible by default.** It is a gambling-adjacent product: surface responsible-gambling context (18+, BeGambleAware), frame predictions as suggestions not guarantees, label ads clearly, and avoid dark patterns that pressure users into wagers.
